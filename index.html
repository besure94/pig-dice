Pig Dice

Let's play a game!

Each player takes turns to roll the dice as many times as they wish. The result of each roll will be added to a running total for each player. If the player rolls a 1, their gained score for their turn will be lost.

  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the other players turn.
  • If the player rolls a number other than 1, it is added to their turn total and the player can continue rolling.
  • If the player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their total score, and it becomes the other players turn.
